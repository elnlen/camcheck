About CamCheck

CamCheck is a free set of browser-based tools built to quickly check whether your webcam is actually working before a call or a stream.

Why I built this

I've lost count of the times I've scrambled right before a meeting wondering "wait, is my camera even on?" — and every time, the fix was either installing some dedicated app or getting bounced between random websites. Opening a browser and knowing in a few seconds whether things look fine felt like it should be enough.

On privacy

Every tool here uses only standard browser APIs — getUserMedia, Canvas — and there's no code anywhere that sends video, photos, or audio to an external server. That's not a policy statement so much as just what the code does; if you're curious, the source is the fastest way to check.

On ads

Right now hosting is cheap enough that ads aren't strictly necessary. That said, I plan to run ads on some pages to support continued development. Ads come through a third-party ad network, so keep in mind that cookies and similar tracking may be used for ad delivery.

What's next

The two things I'd like to prioritize are side-by-side comparison of multiple cameras, and a deeper microphone diagnostic (noise and echo detection). If there's something specific you want, let me know — I'd rather build what people actually ask for than guess at features nobody uses.
