Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

What this site doesn't collect

Every camera- and mic-based tool — Webcam Test, Photo Capture, QR Scanner, Green Screen Test, and the rest — runs entirely on standard browser APIs (getUserMedia / Canvas). Video, photos, and audio are never sent to or stored on a server. There's no account system or login, so no personal information like your name or email address is collected by this site either.

Ad delivery (Google AdSense)

To help cover hosting and development costs, some pages carry ads served through Google AdSense. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies, and may access device information, for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention. This isn't data CamCheck itself collects — it's part of how Google's system works.

  • How Google uses data for ad delivery is explained in Google's policy for partner sites.
  • You can adjust personalized ad settings anytime at Google Ad Settings.

Cookie consent

For visitors in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, and Switzerland, and for visitors in certain US states (such as California), Google AdSense's consent management tool ("Privacy & messaging") shows a consent or opt-out message as required by local law. Visitors elsewhere won't see an additional banner from this mechanism.

You can change your personalized ad settings anytime via Google Ad Settings.

Even with non-personalized ads, a minimal set of cookies may still be used for ad serving, fraud prevention, and frequency capping. If you'd rather avoid cookies entirely, block third-party cookies in your browser settings.

Analytics

No separate analytics tool (like Google Analytics) is installed on this site at this time. AdSense itself may collect aggregate data as part of ad delivery.

Children's privacy

This site isn't directed at children and isn't designed for any specific age group. It does not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Meaningful changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact page.
